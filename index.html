Top 3 Video Games

The Latest and Greatest Video Games From MNRMax

Minecraft

Minecraft is an open sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios. This is in the top 3 because it is one of the few games that you can play for years and still not get bored of.

Portal 2

An old game but nevertheless very fun. The game has a great storyline and the portal mechanics are fun to mess with. After berating the game you can play challenge mode to see how fast you can beat the levels. Portal 1 is also great but I personally enjoyed Portal 2 more.

Valorant

Valorant is a fun game to play with friends, however solo queing is one of the worst things you will experience in any video game. Valorant is a 5v5 tactic shooter with one team trying to plant a bomb and defend it, while the other team trys to defuse the bomb.
